# Property Description Style Guide

## Overview
All AI-generated property descriptions now follow a flowing, single-paragraph format inspired by luxury real estate listings.

## Format Structure

### 2-3 Well-Structured Paragraphs (300-350 words)
The description is organized into clear, flowing paragraphs:

**Paragraph 1: Opening + Interior + Kitchen**
1. **Opening** - Establish ambiance and bedroom/bath count
2. **Entry/Foyer** - First impression details (if applicable)
3. **Living Spaces** - Interior features and materials
4. **Kitchen** - Specific appliances, materials, finishes with brand names

**Paragraph 2: Bedrooms + Outdoor**
5. **Upstairs/Bedrooms** - Primary suite details, flooring, closets
6. **Outdoor Spaces** - Landscaping, amenities, entertainment areas

**Paragraph 3: Closing**
7. **Closing** - Strong statement emphasizing luxury and opportunity
   *(Note: If outdoor section is short, combine paragraphs 2 and 3)*

## Writing Style

### Key Characteristics:
- **One continuous paragraph** (no line breaks)
- **Rich, descriptive language** with specific materials
- **Brand names** when visible (Wolf, SubZero, JennAir, etc.)
- **Detailed descriptions** of each feature
- **Luxury descriptors**: magnificent, stunning, spectacular, remarkable, splendid
- **Flowing transitions** that take the reader through the home

### Example Phrases to Use:
- "masterfully crafted for absolute comfort"
- "captivates with its..."
- "evokes pure tranquility"
- "features [specific brand/material]"
- "drenched in the finest materials"
- "resort-inspired"
- "perfect for entertaining"
- "beyond spectacular opportunity"

## What to INCLUDE:

✅ Bedroom count (e.g., "3-bedroom")  
✅ Bathroom count (e.g., "2-bathroom")  
✅ Property type (home, residence, estate)  
✅ Specific materials (marble, limestone, granite, hardwood)  
✅ Brand names from images (Wolf stove, SubZero fridge, etc.)  
✅ Architectural details (grand staircase, custom cabinetry)  
✅ Finishes and features (wrought iron, glass fronted, custom shelving)  
✅ Outdoor amenities (pool, gazebo, landscaping)  
✅ Generic location terms ("prime location", "exclusive neighborhood")  

## What to EXCLUDE:

❌ Specific street addresses  
❌ City or neighborhood names  
❌ Price or dollar amounts  
❌ Square footage numbers  
❌ ZIP codes  
❌ Lot size numbers  

## Tone Variations

### Professional Tone
**Style**: Refined, descriptive, suitable for high-end MLS listings  
**Language**: "masterfully crafted", "captivates with", "showcases"  
**Focus**: Materials, craftsmanship, elegant details

**Example Opening**:
> This magnificent 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom residence has been masterfully crafted for absolute comfort and sophistication. The home captivates with custom cabinetry, premium hardwood flooring, and soaring ceilings, creating an environment of refined elegance...

### Casual Tone
**Style**: Warm, enthusiastic, but still descriptive  
**Language**: "you'll notice", "perfect for", "gives you"  
**Focus**: Lifestyle benefits while maintaining quality descriptions

**Example Opening**:
> Welcome to this amazing 4-bed, 3-bath home that's been thoughtfully designed for modern living! From the moment you step inside, you'll notice the beautiful hardwood floors and custom details, making this space perfect for both everyday life and entertaining...

### Luxury Tone
**Style**: Extremely elegant, sophisticated, prestige-focused  
**Language**: "evokes pure tranquility", "drenched in the finest", "unparalleled"  
**Focus**: Exclusivity, premium materials, exceptional quality

**Example Opening**:
> This magnificent 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom masterpiece has been masterfully crafted for absolute glamour and comfort. The home captivates with its custom cabinetry featuring exquisite finishes, premium hardwood flooring, and a spectacular chandelier that dazzles...

## Example Full Description (Luxury Tone)

**Paragraph 1:**
> This magnificent 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom residence has been masterfully crafted for absolute comfort and sophistication. The home captivates with custom cabinetry featuring beautiful finishes, premium hardwood flooring throughout, and ample windows accompanied by custom wooden shutters to provide access to abundant natural light when desired. The state of the art kitchen features professional-grade stainless steel appliances, stunning granite countertops, and a glass-fronted wine refrigerator, offering the utmost in culinary convenience and style.

**Paragraph 2:**
> Upstairs, the primary suite evokes pure tranquility with its splendid dark wood flooring, substantial walk-in closet with custom shelving, and spa-like en-suite bathroom featuring dual vanities and a soaking tub. Outside, the resort-inspired backyard presents lush landscaping with mature trees, a covered patio with outdoor kitchen perfect for entertaining, and thoughtfully designed lighting throughout.

**Paragraph 3:**
> Drenched in the finest materials and appointments, this is a beyond spectacular opportunity in an exclusive neighborhood.

## Technical Implementation

### OpenAI Prompt Instructions:
- Generate 300-350 words in 2-3 well-structured paragraphs
- Paragraph 1: opening → entry → living → kitchen
- Paragraph 2: upstairs/bedrooms → outdoor
- Paragraph 3: closing statement
- Use specific materials and brand names from image analysis
- Match the luxury writing style with flowing transitions
- Separate paragraphs with double line breaks

### Fallback Templates:
- Generate 2-3 paragraph descriptions
- Follow same logical flow through the home
- Use image analysis data to populate specific features
- Maintain consistent luxury language
- Each paragraph focused on specific areas

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing a description, verify:

- [ ] 2-3 paragraph format with clear breaks
- [ ] 300-350 words in length
- [ ] Flows from entrance → indoor → outdoor → closing
- [ ] Uses specific materials/brands from images
- [ ] Includes luxury descriptors (magnificent, stunning, etc.)
- [ ] NO address, city, price, or square footage
- [ ] NO furniture or staging items mentioned
- [ ] Features are grouped logically by area
- [ ] Creates a narrative journey through the home
- [ ] Strong closing statement in final paragraph

---

**This style ensures every property description reads like a high-end luxury listing, regardless of property type or price point.**
